Bio

I am a biomedical engineer turned machine learning (ML) engineer with experience building ML systems since 2018. I am grateful to have worked at great academic labs like TERI, IGIB, and IGBMC. My work revolved around building statistical and machine learning models in healthcare and biological sciences (genomics, proteomics, etc).

As a freelance consultant, I've worked in the patenting, e-commerce analytics, and SaaS industries.

Some of my work:

E-commerce: Systems for named entity extraction, information organization, product embeddings, information retrieval systems like semantic search, RAG, and low latency extreme classification tasks.

SaaS: Low latency (sub 100 ms) task-specific ML systems like classifiers for both image and text modalities, custom embedding models, and information extraction systems.

Patenting: Evaluated the feasibility of AI systems for patent-related applications.
