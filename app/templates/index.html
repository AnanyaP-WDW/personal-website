About me

I am a biomedical engineer turned machine learning (ML) engineer with experience building ML systems since 2018. I have worked at great academic labs like TERI, IGIB, and IGBMC. My work revolved around building statistical and machine learning models in healthcare and biological sciences (genomics, proteomics, etc).

As a freelance consultant, I've worked in the patenting, e-commerce analytics, and SaaS industries.

E-commerce: Systems for named entity extraction, information organization, product embeddings, information retrieval systems like semantic search, RAG, and low latency extreme classification tasks.
